Your Visit

Come for the Coffee. Stay for the Cats.

Whether you're looking for a quiet place to enjoy a handcrafted latte, spend time with adoptable rescue cats, or simply relax by the shore, RipVanWhisker is designed to feel like your favorite neighborhood café.

☕ Sip

Enjoy freshly brewed coffee, specialty espresso drinks, tea, pastries, and seasonal treats in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

🐾 Connect

Visit our cat lounge to meet playful rescue cats, learn their stories, and experience the joy they bring every day.

❤️ Change a Life

Every drink, every purchase, and every visit helps support rescue, enrichment, and adoption efforts for cats in our community.

More Than a Café

RipVanWhisker is a place to slow down, reconnect, and enjoy a unique experience that blends exceptional coffee with compassion for rescue cats.

Some guests come for a quiet afternoon. Others come to meet adoptable cats. Many leave with both a favorite coffee and a favorite feline.
