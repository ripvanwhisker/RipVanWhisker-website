Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Want to Know

We're excited to welcome you to Atlantic City's first cat café. Here are answers to some of the questions we hear most often.

How does a cat café work?

Enjoy coffee and refreshments in our café, then spend time with our adoptable rescue cats in the dedicated cat lounge. The café and cat lounge are designed to provide a comfortable experience for both guests and cats.

Do I need a reservation?

Reservation details will be announced closer to opening. We expect to offer both walk-in availability and online reservations for the cat lounge.

Can I adopt one of the cats?

Absolutely! Our goal is to help rescue cats find loving forever homes through partnerships with local rescue organizations. Adoption requirements will be managed by our rescue partners.

Are children welcome?

Yes! Families are welcome. Some areas or activities may have age guidelines to help ensure the comfort and safety of both children and cats.

Are the food and cat areas separated?

Yes. The café and cat lounge are separate spaces designed to meet health regulations while providing a safe and enjoyable experience.

How can I support RipVanWhisker before opening?

You can become a Founding Human, purchase merchandise, share our story on social media, or simply follow our progress as we prepare to open our doors.

Where does my purchase go?

Support from merchandise, sponsorships, and future café visits helps build RipVanWhisker while supporting rescue, enrichment, and adoption efforts.

Still Have Questions?

We're always happy to help. Reach out anytime and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
