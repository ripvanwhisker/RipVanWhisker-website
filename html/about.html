ABOUT RIPVANWHISKER

More Than a Cat Café.
A Home for Community, Coffee & Second Chances.

RipVanWhisker Cat Café was inspired by one simple belief: every rescue cat deserves a second chance, and every community deserves a place that feels like home. We're creating a welcoming destination where handcrafted coffee, meaningful connections, and adoptable cats come together in the heart of Atlantic City.

OUR STORY

Building Something Worth Coming Back To

RipVanWhisker began with a vision to create Atlantic City's first cat café—a place where rescue cats, coffee lovers, families, and visitors all feel welcome.

Atlantic City has always been known for unforgettable experiences. We believe it also deserves a destination dedicated to comfort, compassion, and connection. A place where slowing down is encouraged, conversations happen naturally, and rescue cats can begin new chapters with loving families.

Every purchase, every founding supporter, and every shared story helps move that vision one step closer to reality.

We're not simply opening a café—we're building a place our community will be proud to call its own.
