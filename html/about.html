ABOUT RIPVANWHISKER

More Than a Cat Café.

A Place Where Community, Coffee & Second Chances Come Together.

RipVanWhisker Cat Café is being created with one simple mission: to build a welcoming destination where rescue cats find loving homes, neighbors become friends, and every visit makes a difference. Inspired by Atlantic City's energy and community spirit, we're creating a place unlike anything else on the Boardwalk.

OUR STORY

Building Something Worth Coming Back To

RipVanWhisker started with the belief that a great cup of coffee can bring people together—and that rescue cats deserve every opportunity to find a forever home.

Atlantic City is known for excitement, entertainment, and unforgettable experiences. We wanted to add something different: a peaceful place where guests can slow down, enjoy handcrafted drinks, meet adoptable cats, and spend time with the people they care about.

Every founding supporter, every piece of merchandise, and every visit helps us build a permanent home where rescue organizations, local residents, visitors, and cats all benefit together.
