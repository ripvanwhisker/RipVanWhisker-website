About Us

More Than a Cat Café

RipVanWhisker Cat Café is being built on a simple belief: great coffee, genuine community, and rescue cats can change lives— one visit at a time.

Our Story

RipVanWhisker began with a vision of creating a welcoming place where people can slow down, enjoy exceptional coffee, and experience the joy of spending time with rescue cats. Every detail is designed to create comfort, connection, and compassion.

We believe a neighborhood café can be more than somewhere to grab a drink—it can become a gathering place where friendships begin, families create memories, and animals find loving forever homes.

🐾 Why Rescue Cats?

Every cat deserves a second chance. By partnering with rescue organizations, we hope to increase adoptions while giving guests the opportunity to meet cats in a relaxed, home-like environment.

🌊 Why Atlantic City?

Atlantic City is known for its Boardwalk, entertainment, and rich history. We're excited to add something different—a cozy destination where residents and visitors alike can enjoy coffee, community, and the companionship of rescue cats.

❤️ Our Mission

Build a welcoming community, support rescue efforts, serve exceptional coffee, and help every cat find the loving home it deserves.

Looking Ahead

The doors haven't opened yet, but the journey has already begun. Every Founding Human, every piece of merchandise, and every word of encouragement helps bring RipVanWhisker closer to welcoming its first guests—and celebrating its first adoption.
